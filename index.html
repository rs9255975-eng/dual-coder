Features

EVERYTHING A FOUNDER NEEDS

From idea submission to market analysis — all in one clean dashboard.

Quick Idea Submission

Submit your startup idea in under 2 minutes. Fill in the name, description, category, and key metrics — and it's live instantly.

Market Potential Score

Each idea gets a market potential score from 1–10 based on your input.

Build Difficulty Meter

Rate how technically complex your idea is to build. Helps other founders understand the effort required.
